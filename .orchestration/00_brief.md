# 00 Brief — AI Ethics Quest 3D

## Clarified brief
- Target: 초등 고학년
- Implementation: Three.js browser 3D game, deployable to GitHub Pages
- AI ethics topics: 개인정보, 편향, 저작권, 딥페이크
- Zelda-like scope: exploration + shrine puzzle 중심
- Deliverables: game + GitHub Pages deployment + presentation draft; include 2-class lesson flow, activity sheet, and rubric because the game is for class use.

## Product concept
**Title:** AI 윤리의 섬: 알고리즘의 신전

A small Zelda-inspired 3D learning island. Students play as an “윤리 수호자” exploring a compact open-world island. Four zones introduce AI ethics problems through NPCs and shrine puzzles:
1. 개인정보 마을
2. 편향의 숲
3. 저작권 유적
4. 딥페이크 동굴

The player collects four “윤리 조각” and unlocks a final AI Core decision that summarizes the concepts for a two-class lesson.

## Scope guard
This is not a full AAA open-world game. It is a 2-class educational 3D web prototype with a small island, readable objectives, simple movement, NPC interactions, shrine puzzles, progress tracking, and teacher-facing documents.
